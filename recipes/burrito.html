Burrito

Description

A burrito is a soft flour tortilla wrapped around a flavorful filling such as seasoned meat, beans, rice, cheese, and fresh vegetables.

Ingridients

  • Tortillas: large8-10 inch
  • Seasoned Rice,Beans,Meat or plant based proteins like paneer,tofu
  • Vegetables
  • Salsas
  • Cheeses: Shredded cheddar, Monterey Jack, mozzarella, or queso fresco.
  • Creamy Elements: Guacamole, sour cream, or crema.

Steps

  1. Warm a large tortilla.
  2. Cook and season the meat (beef, chicken, or another filling).
  3. Prepare the rice according to package instructions.
  4. Heat the beans until warm
  5. Place the tortilla flat on a clean surface.
  6. Add a layer of rice in the center of the tortilla.
  7. Spoon the beans over the rice.
  8. Add the cooked meat on top.
  9. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the filling.
  10. Add toppings such as lettuce, tomatoes, salsa, or sour cream.
  11. Fold the sides of the tortilla inward.
  12. Roll the tortilla tightly from the bottom to form a burrito.
  13. Toast the burrito in a skillet for 1-2 minutes per side (optional).
  14. Serve warm and enjoy. 🌯
