Lasagna

Steps to make Lasagna

  • Meat: 1 pound of ground meat (usually a mix of \(\frac{1}{2}\) pound lean ground beef and \(\frac{1}{2}\) pound Italian sausage).
  • Aromatics: 1 diced yellow onion and 2 cloves of minced garlic.
  • Tomatoes: 24 to 28 ounces of high-quality marinara or pasta sauce, supplemented with \(\frac{1}{4}\) cup of tomato paste for a thicker, richer base.
